Realtime Map Assignment

The Google Map below is enhanced with markers showing where traffic incidents are located using data parsed from the traffic API by Microsoft. Any traffic incident that is between Los Angeles, CA and Irvine, CA should appear on the map. The marker changes to a do not enter sign if the road is closed. A heatmap is used to show areas where there are many traffic incidents occurring at that time. Clicking on the coordinates will recenter the map to the marker where the traffic incident is occurring.
